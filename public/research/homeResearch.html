Research

If you want to help reshape the future of humanity, you first need to connect people whose concern is the human condition. This conviction forms the basis of research and teaching at the Research, Arts and Language (RAL) Institute.
RAL Institute is a private international research framework, a platform of exchange, collaboration and promotion designed to welcome researchers with great ambition and commitment, regardless of their nationality.
This framework is therefore open to any scholar who shares our conviction that change and progress only come about when people have a fundamental understanding of the world around us and of the factors generating and maintaining systems and conditions of poverty and enslavement.
Members of RAL Institute conduct both disciplinary and interdisciplinary research covering broad areas of knowledge to deliver informed understanding and/or solutions to societal problems across all academic fields.
After three decades in which globalization trended, humanity has entered a new phase, a phase of crises: crisis of democracy, crisis of systems, racial crisis, pandemic crisis, economic crisis, cultural crisis, scientific crisis, etc. In the middle of these crises, authoritarianism and totalitarianism seem to have had a makeover to thrive. At the same time, many existing issues including food and nutrition issues, economic issues, health issues, environmental issues, energy issues, financial issues, housing issues, security issues, sociocultural issues, etc get worse and worse.
It is our responsibility as researchers to guide humanity through this difficult phase. Scholars on this platform work for various universities and primarily come here to design and implement research projects, exchange, collaborate and promote each other’s research outputs.
