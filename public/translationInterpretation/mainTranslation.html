Our Services

We use general translation to translate documents including:
  • - Letters and emails
  • - General business documents
  • - Job applications and CVs
  • - Birth and marriage certificates
  • - Passports and driving licenses
  • - Brochures and catalogues
  • - Journalistic texts
  • - Advertisements
  • - Information on art and culture
  • - Texts about tourism and gastronomy

We translate the following documents with their own language of jargonistic expertise:
  • legal documents: contracts, court documents, mergers and acquisitions (M&A) documents, litigation documents, immigration documents, etc.
  • medical documents: models of care, consent forms, medical activity waivers, discharge instructions, complaint forms, etc.
  • academic documents: research proposals, research papers, articles, theses, scholarly publications, etc.
  • financial documents: financial statements, banking documents, financial plans/reports, etc.
  • development project documents: project proposals/charters, project financial plans/reports, quaterly/annual plans/reports, flagship reports, briefings, teaching and training material, etc.
  • institutional documents: annual reports, press releases, procedure manuals, code of conducts, other corporate documents.

We provide translation for technical documents such as:
  • user manuals: maintenance manuals, repair manuals, user guides, instructions, etc.
  • product specifications: product requirements, functional requirements, etc.
  • technical manuals: application programming interface (API) documentation, policy manuals, etc.
  • machinery instructions: product descriptions, operation and service manuals, patents, regulatory documents, guidelines, etc.
  • material safety data sheets: chemical products data sheets, etc.
  • software development documentation: architecture descriptions, program source code, tutorials, user guides, troubleshooting manuals, installation instructions, etc.
