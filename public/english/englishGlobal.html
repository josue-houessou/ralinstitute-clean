WHO IS THIS COURSE FOR?

Our Global English course is designed for junior and adult learners who would like to widen their skills and develop a good command of English. You are also in the right place if you want to start from scratch.

WHAT IS THIS COURSE ABOUT?

  • Methodical language education focusing on linguistic (listening, reading, speaking and writing) competences, interactive communication and discourse types.
  • Effective communication strategies and techniques for different situations, settings and audiences.
  • Integrated 21st-century information, technology, creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills into language instruction.
  • Potential completion of 3 CEFR levels in just 12 months with our year-round program.

HOW CAN OUR COURSE TRANSFORM YOUR ENGLISH SKILLS?

  • We pinpoint your English language problems.
  • We provide reasearch-based solutions.
  • We boost your vocabulary and grammar.

  • We spot your mistakes and needs.
  • We literally upgrade your pronunciation.
  • We help you create and install English language habits.
