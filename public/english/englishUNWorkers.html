UN Workers

English is:

  • - one of the six official languages of the UN.
  • - one of its two working languages.
  • - the language of diplomacy globally.

WHO IS THIS COURSE FOR?

This high-profile English program is designed to help you achieve your communication goals as:

  • a staff member of one of these UN funds and programs: UNAIDS, UNCC, UNCTAD, UNDP, UNECE, UNEP, UNESCO, UNHCR, UNCEF, UNIDIR, UNIDO, UNITAR, UNOPS, UNRISD, UNWOMEN, WFP, WHO, WIPO, WMO and WTO.
  • staff of UN permanent missions, including observer mission.
  • staff of service providers.
  • Employees of NGOs accredited to the UN.
  • Press corps.
  • GAVI (Vaccine Alliance).
  • WEF (World Economic Forum).
  • Consultants working with the UN organizations and agencies.
  • Other Multilateral International Organizations.
  • International Non-Governmental Organizations.

WHAT IS THIS COURSE ABOUT?

  • English language integrated-skills: linguistic, sociolinguistic, and discursive.
  • Communication skills in personal, public and professional contexts.
  • English language for diplomacy, and as a working language.
  • Techniques & strategies for top preparedness for UN Language Proficiency Examination.

HOW CAN OUR COURSE TRANSFORM YOUR ENGLISH SKILLS?

  • We pinpoint your English language problems.
  • We provide reasearch-based solutions.
  • We boost your vocabulary and grammar.

  • We spot your mistakes and needs.
  • We literally upgrade your pronunciation.
  • We help you create and install English language habits.
