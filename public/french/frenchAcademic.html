Academic French

WHO IS THIS COURSE FOR?

Our French for Academic Purposes (FAP) training program is intended to help researchers, academics, and students in all fields of academic study and research achieve proficiency in French. This program aims to establish you in full control of your career as a researcher with a great command and confident use of the French language.

WHAT YOU WILL LEARN!

You will learn:

  1. to read fast and to use this skill to boost your research project and academic career;
  2. to write in a high-quality academic register and be made aware of journal guidelines and submission requirements;
  3. to make powerful scientific presentations using diagrams, graphs, and tables to make a point;
  4. to refine your academic skills and upgrade your abilities to interpret, evaluate, analyze, criticize, discuss, and explain in French.

CERTIFICATIONS!

Upon completion of each class level, you will get our Certificate of Completion of the French for
Academic Purposes for B2, C1, or C2.
