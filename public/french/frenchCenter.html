Our certifications and international tests

We offer a wide choice of high-quality certification options. Likewise, we prepare learners for internationally established French language diplomas including:

  • DELF (Diplôme d'études en langue Française)
  • DALF (Diplôme Approfondi de langue Française)
  • DILF (Diplôme Initial de Langue Française)

We can help you if you need French:

  • - to further your studies at home or abroad,
  • - to write your research papers,
  • - to thrive in your work or business,
  • - to make the most of job offers or travel opportunities,
  • - to make your presentations in conferences, seminars and workshops.
