Business

Explore and conquer French speaking markets globally with confidence. Our Business French program is tailored for English speaking business men and women aiming to break language barriers and seize new business opportunities.
The program is designed to help you elevate your communication skills, refine your negotiation language skills, and expand your client base globally.
You will be able to interact with much ease with your French speaking colleagues, collaborators and clients using the right language to express your mind. It is a needs-based course.
Our team combines highly qualified business experts and language instructors devoted to making your learning journey an inspiring experience.

WHOM IS THIS COURSE FOR?
  • Business Executives & Leaders
  • Entrepreneurs
  • Business Professionals seeking to enhance their language skills
  • English speakers aiming to expand into the French-speaking business world
WHAT WILL YOU LEARN?
  • Pronunciation and accent
  • Listening, Reading & Speaking skills
  • Telephone & Email French
  • Sector-specific terminology
  • Presentation & Negotiation language skills
KEY BENEFITS
  • Be able to communicate in French in any business negotiation, business meeting, conference, seminar, workshop
  • Develop an excellent command of French
  • Become part of global peer networks
ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Classes are offered at three levels: B2 (Upper-Intermediate), C1 (Advanced), and C2 (Proficiency). You must have successfully completed level B1 or be classified into B2 by placement test results.
