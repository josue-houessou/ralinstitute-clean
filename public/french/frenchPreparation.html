DALF

DALF is a diploma for the advanced levels (C1, and C2) of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, delivered by the French Ministry of National Education.

DELF

DELF is a diploma for A1, A2, B1, and B2 levels of the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages, delivered by the French Ministry of National Education.

DILF

DILF is the first French diploma that sanctions basic French level (A1.1) required for mobility.
