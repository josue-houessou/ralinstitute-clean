RAL Institute celebrates students achievements.

by Paul-Marie Houessou

Ten B1 and forty A2 students of the University of Abomey-Calavi (UAC) who completed the 2024 Pro-RUWA sponsored English Proficiency Summer Training were celebrated through a certificate award ceremony on Saturday, December 14th.

This second edition of the Pro-RUWA-sponsored summer training ran from July to September.Most of the learners at this edition came from the Faculty of Agricultural Sciences of the University of Abomey-Calavi. But students from other faculties including the Faculty of Economic and Management Sciences (FASEG), the National Institute of Water (INE) and the English Departement of the University College of Adjarra were also involved.

The certificate award ceremony was presided over by the Vice-Dean of the Faculty of Agricultural Sciences, Prof. Augustin AOUDJI. Prof. Luc Hypolite Dossa, coordinator of the "Promoting Academic Capacities for Sustainable Agricultural Resources Use in West Africa", also attended the ceremony. He reiterated his commitment to keep working for the academic capacity development of students.
