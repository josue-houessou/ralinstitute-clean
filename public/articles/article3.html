A unique English teaching method

by Latifou Orou Adama
07 September 2025
Abomey-Calavi, Benin



Latifou is currently taking our level B1 course of the English Proficiency Summer Training (EPST) 2025 after successfully completing the level A2 course. He has seen tremendous improvements in his English skills since joining our classes. In this short text, he tells us how the teaching method has helped him make quick progress.


Our main English instructor teaches with passion and a clear sense of purpose. He always smiles when he enters the classroom, and his smile creates a warm and pleasant atmosphere that immediately puts us at ease. His way of teaching is simple yet effective, because he explains every lesson with practical examples that we can easily understand and remember. He uses stories, real-life situations, and interactive exercises that keep our attention alive.

His pedagogy is unique because it combines discipline with kindness. He listens carefully to our questions, encourages us when we hesitate, and corrects our mistakes with patience. He never judges us, but instead he motivates us to try again. His qualities as a teacher—his humility, his simplicity, his clarity, and his dedication—make him a true model for us. The entire class feels happy and motivated to learn whenever he is present. His energy fills the classroom with life, and we all participate with enthusiasm. The lessons are never boring because he makes them dynamic and interactive. We enjoy working in groups, practicing dialogues, and answering his questions.

He always finds a way to make every student feel important. Because of him, we develop confidence in speaking and writing. The atmosphere in the class is joyful, friendly, and supportive, and this makes learning English not only a school subject but also a pleasant experience. We laugh together, we share ideas, and we grow as learners. Every student feels that English becomes easier and more interesting thanks to his teaching style. Personally, I feel deeply motivated by the way he teaches. He always pushes me to give the best of myself. His humility and simplicity inspire me to work harder and to believe in my abilities. He helps me improve my pronunciation by showing me how to produce sounds correctly.

He teaches me how to identify stressed and unstressed syllables, and how to recognize the different vowel sounds in each syllable. He explains grammar rules in a clear and memorable way, and he gives me exercises that strengthen my skills. Little by little, he makes me speak English with more confidence, and he guides me to sound more like a native speaker. Thanks to him, I discover not only the rules of English but also the joy of expressing myself in this beautiful language.
